Boujee Boulder Holder

This dapper little guy sits the Southernmost of all the little fellas, on a small concrete wall. He wears a full suit and top hat, and of course a bowtie. In his lap, he holds a rock the size of his torso. And he looks off into the distance. He really is just a cool dude.

Wonder Woman

Sitting on a large stone near the middle of the courtyard between Arntzen and the Biology building, you will see this lady. Above her head she holds a massive boulder double her size. She wears a bun in her hair and a dress. She is an absolute GirlBoss

Teamwork! (And Leadership)

Three men can be found hoisting a massive stone above the ground. But if you look closely, you might see the much smaller woman upon the rock. Seemingly looking down at one of the struggling fellas with her hands on her hips. And even more curious, she sits on a coin, that is between her and the rock.

Sleeping in the Sun

Over one of the small concrete walls, one of the figures appears to be sleeping, or at least shielding his eyes from the sunlight. His shoes are taken off and set next to him. And even his little top hot is resting along next to him. If he is trying to just get the sun out of his eyes, which I assume to be the feat of strength, he would be a lot smarter to move under the tress right behind him.

Don't Let Go

Here on the Northern side of this exhibit, a girl in a dress, heels, and a hat, is holding up a large flat rock overhanging the rest. Her head is cocked back, indicating it might be heavy for her. But she keeps holding on! Not letting that stone fall.
